I a fast-paced and inter-connected world, the values of authenticity and longevity become challenged. What is ‘Peruviannes’ in this exponentially globalized planet? The millenary craft traditions from women in Peru are the source of inspiration for Andrea Jose’s creative work. Andrea Jose travels through the materiality, craftsmanship and traditions of Peru to inspire and make her collections in collaboration with other Peruvian women.

ANDREA JOSE

Working in social development with mining projects whilst learning jewellery techniques with master artisans Andrea has strengthened the connection to her country, honing her sensibilities towards material and craft. The particularly male dominated mining industry inspired and motivated her to value the work of strong female leaders. After three years of working at her own jewellery company, the first B Corp in the jewellery sector in Latin America, and being trained at the MA Design in Central Saint Martins, Andrea Jose bases her practice through a collaborative work that engages and transforms.

Through these diverse experiences which have forged her professional life she has envisioned a new approach towards the way we produce and consume jewellery.
